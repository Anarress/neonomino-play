Neonomino — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-14 · Contact: esatelmas@gmail.com

Neonomino is a puzzle game by Anarress. It is designed to work without collecting personal information. This page explains the little data it touches.

What we do NOT do

  • No account or sign-up. No email, name, or contact information is requested.
  • No advertising SDKs, no ad tracking, no sale of data. The game shows no forced ads.
  • No cookies for tracking. No fingerprinting.
  • No access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files.

Data stored on your device only

Your scores, streaks, campaign progress, settings, and unlocked cosmetics are stored in your browser's / the app's local storage on your device. They never leave it except as described below, and uninstalling the app (or clearing site data) deletes them.

Leaderboards (optional)

If you finish a Daily Circuit, the game submits your result to our leaderboard server (Cloudflare Workers): a randomly generated player ID (not linked to your identity), the nickname you choose, your score, and your combo peak. Nicknames are public on the leaderboard. To have an entry removed, email us the nickname and day number.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics service, to count pageviews and anonymous gameplay events (e.g. "a run ended"). No personal data, no cross-site tracking, no advertising use.

Children

The game collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be published at this address.
